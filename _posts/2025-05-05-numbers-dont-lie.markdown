## 2. The best indicator for quality of life?

**Infant Mortality Rate (IMR)** â€“ The number of deaths during the first year of life that takes place per 1,000 live births.
> Infant mortality is such a powerful indicator because low rates are impossible to achieve without having a combination of several critical conditions that define good quality of life â€“ good healthcare in general; adequate and sanitary living conditions; and access to social support for disadvantaged families.
